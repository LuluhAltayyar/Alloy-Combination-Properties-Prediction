About


We predict the features' percentages of different combinations of the alloys based on the different numbers of each element within the alloy combination.

What is the alloy?
Alloys are solid combinations of mixtures of metal or of metals and nonmetallic elements that have desirable and distinctive properties.

Why a website for alloy combination properties prediction?
Scientists could simulate the combining process and predict the features' percentages of the new resulting compounds with higher performance prediction and without even wasting scarce materials or take the risk when dealing with dangerous ones.
